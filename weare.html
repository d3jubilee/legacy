TEAM Here we are

D3 has multi-located offices in Seoul, Korea and Palo Alto, USA. Our team work with international and domestic social entrepreneur and investor to pursue "making better world".

Team
Doug Duckjun LEE
CEO | USA & Korea

Doug is an angel investor in Korea and the San Francisco bay area. He moved to the space of impact investment in 2010. He is a former CFO of Gmarket (acquired by eBay). Prior to Gmarket, he was an investment banker with Schroders, Citigroup and Credit Suisse. He holds a master's degree in Accounting and Finance from London School of Economics and a bachelor's degree from Seoul National University.

Jiyoung Lee
Principal | USA

Jiyoung's professional ambitions have always been to strike a balance between social responsibility and business profitability. She believes impact investment is an effective means for helping socially responsible businesses thrive, making our world a better place. Prior to joining D3, Jiyoung founded NADOO, a lifestyle sharing platform with e-commerce engine. Prior to NADOO, she worked as analytics specialist at ZeroDesktop, a San Mateo based cloud technology startup. She has also worked in fund sales and securities structuring roles at Goldman Sachs Asset Management in Korea and Merrill Lynch in Hong Kong. She started her career at Kookmin Bank. Jiyoung received her MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business and a bachelor's degree from Korea University. She is also a CFA charterholder.
