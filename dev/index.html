D3 | Redefine Growth

We aim to redefine what growth means in our society for our Change Maker and move forward by creating socially meaningful impact.

impact | Investment with Purpose

Some believe that impact investing is the evolutionary conclusion of heartless capitalism and heavy-handed government spending. We do, however, believe that investors around the world have a responsibility to look beyond straight financial returns to examine the externalities their investments are helping to scale. Every case of exploitation and injustice in the world is the result of narrow decision-making criteria. We invite investors and entrepreneurs around the world to expand the goals of their businesses or their investments so that we can bring about a sustainable, equitable legacy for our children.
